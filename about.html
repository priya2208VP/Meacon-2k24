ABOUT US


MEACON in its eleventh triumphant year, exemplifies excellence in the Department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering.

With an average attendance exceeding 6000 students nationwide, MEACON is a testament to our commitment to innovation. Organized by and for students, it provides a grand stage for showcasing talents and ideas, particularly in the specialized field of instrumentation.From groundbreaking sensor technologies to precision measurements, MEACON explores the forefront of advancements in this domain.

The symposium fosters a peer platform for innovation, creating an intellectually stimulating and entertaining environment. Partnering with sponsors, MEACON consistently captivates attendees, leaving a lasting impression of excellence.
